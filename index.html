I'm a computer science student who loves building things with code.

My name is Quentin Chu, and I am currently studying computer science with a strong focus on artificial intelligence, software development, and product design. I am passionate about understanding how things work under the hood, and I enjoy turning ideas into real, working software that people can actually use.

Python is my primary programming language, and I have used it to build several personal projects ranging from automation scripts and data processing tools to small AI-powered applications. Working through these projects has taught me how to structure code cleanly, debug effectively, and think through problems from a user's perspective before jumping into implementation.

I am especially interested in AI tools for education, productivity, research, and creative work. I like products that make complex information easier to use without hiding the important details. Beyond technical skills, I care about clear communication, thoughtful design, and writing code that others can read and maintain.

Outside of coursework, I am always looking for new challenges to push myself further as a developer. Whether it is exploring a new framework, contributing to a team project, or diving into a topic I have never seen before, I thrive on continuous learning. I am currently seeking internship and early career opportunities where I can grow alongside experienced engineers and contribute to meaningful products.
